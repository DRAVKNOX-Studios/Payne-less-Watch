Payne-less : Watch is a lightweight, open-source clock app with no ads, no analytics, and no internet permissions.

Features:
• Alarms - repeating days, custom ringtone, gradual volume ramp, snooze, label and notes
• Timers - multiple concurrent timers with foreground service notification
• Stopwatch - lap tracking with foreground service
• World Clock - track time across multiple time zones
• Home screen widget - live clock with next alarm display, fully themeable
• Full custom theming - choose your own background and accent colours
• Missed alarm notifications with reschedule on boot, timezone change, and app update

Privacy:
Payne-less : Watch stores all data locally on your device. No data is collected, transmitted, or shared.
No network permissions are requested.
